It takes time, space and trust to make lasting change. At Cyrenians, we take that time, we hold that space, and we build that trust, working with people on their own terms for however long it takes.�

  • Trust and support – we offer ongoing support without deadlines or conditions

  • A safe place to stay – no curfews, no shared dorms, and no getting locked in or out at night� 
  • A positive future – trust, care and community to people who’ve been struggling alone for too long
